# Task 5 - Part B: Stay Safe Online Checklist

As a Digital Ambassador, I recommend the following 8 actionable tips for college students in India:

1. **UPI Safety:** Never enter your UPI PIN to "receive" money; a PIN is only required to send or pay money[cite: 83].
2. **QR Code Caution:** Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals on WhatsApp or marketplace apps[cite: 83].
3. **Link Verification:** Avoid clicking on "urgent" links in SMS or emails claiming your bank account or KYC is suspended[cite: 77].
4. **Strong Passwords:** Use unique, complex passwords for your university portal and email accounts.
5. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on all social media and banking apps for an extra layer of security.
6. **Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid logging into banking apps or making purchases while connected to public or campus-guest Wi-Fi.
7. **App Permissions:** Regularly check your mobile app permissions and revoke access to your camera, mic, or contacts for unnecessary apps.
8. **Verify Identity:** If a friend asks for money via social media, always call them directly to verify it isn't a hacked account.
