# Task 4 - Part B: Social Media Do's and Don'ts

As a college student and Digital Ambassador, maintaining a responsible online presence is essential. Here is my checklist for professional social media use:

### 5 Do's:
1. **Do** use privacy settings to control who can see your personal information and posts.
2. **Do** think twice before posting; ensure your content reflects your professional goals.
3. **Do** engage in positive and constructive discussions within your academic community.
4. **Do** verify information from credible sources before sharing it with others.
5. **Do** use a professional profile picture and clear bio on platforms like LinkedIn.

### 5 Don'ts:
1. **Don't** post offensive, discriminatory, or disrespectful comments or media.
2. **Don't** share sensitive personal data like your address, phone number, or OTPs.
3. **Don't** engage with "trolls" or participate in online cyberbullying.
4. **Don't** post content that depicts illegal activities or violates university policies.
5. **Don't** complain about professors, classmates, or employers on public platforms.
